Tubestone
Tubestone provides faster Youtube experiance by fetching only the video and removes the elements that usually blocks the videos.
Tubestone also provides shortcuts for video control with the keyboards.
Related Videos button displays videos that are related to the one that is currently playing.
f Full Screen
r Replay the video
t Switch between search and related videos
1 Rewind 10 seconds
2 Rewind 5 seconds
3 Fast forward 5 seconds
4 Fast forward 10 seconds
q Increase the volume by 5
a Decrease the volume by 5
z Mute
